Our App contains a health chat function powered by third-party artificial intelligence services, to provide general daily health and wellness suggestions.

  3. Data Usage Limitation The shared chat content is only used to provide you with chat replies and health consultation services. It shall not be used for advertising, marketing, commercial promotion or other unrelated purposes.
  4. User Consent & Control Before using the health chat function, users must give explicit consent via in-app pop-up authorization. You may disable the health chat function at any time in the App settings. After being disabled, the AI chat service will stop, and no further chat content will be uploaded or shared.
