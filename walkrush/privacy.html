Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use the WalkRush: Steps Into Wins mobile application (the “Service”). By downloading, installing, or using the Service, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy. We only use your information to provide, maintain, improve, and secure the Service. We will not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as described in this document.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate and improve the walking challenge and reward experience:

•                      Account Information: When you log in via Google, Apple ID, or other supported platforms, we receive your email address, display name, and profile picture (if available). This is used to create your account, sync step data across devices, and verify your identity.

•                      Advertising ID (IDFA / GAID): We collect your device’s advertising identifier to prevent fraud, improve ad quality, and deliver relevant in-app offers and rewards.

•                      IP Address: Used to determine general geographic region, enhance service security, prevent abuse, and meet legal requirements.

•                      Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, system language, time zone, battery status, network type (Wi‑Fi / cellular), and session start/end times.

•                      Activity & Usage Data: We record daily step counts, challenge completion, goal progress, reward status, feature usage, and session duration. This helps optimize motivation features and personalize your experience.

•                      Crash & Performance Logs: When the app errors or crashes, we collect diagnostic logs to fix bugs, improve stability, and enhance performance.

•                      Installed Apps (with consent): Our integrated ad SDK may request access to your installed apps list on first launch to improve ad relevance. You may decline this permission without affecting core step-tracking functions. We do not store or process this data.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for the following purposes:

•                      Operate and maintain the Service, including step tracking, challenge management, and reward processing.

•                      Personalize your experience, suggest daily goals, and deliver relevant content.

•                      Display personalized advertisements and optional reward tasks.

•                      Respond to your support inquiries and account requests.

•                      Send important updates about the app, policies, or terms.

•                      Detect fraud, protect security, and prevent abuse of reward systems.

•                      Analyze usage trends to improve features, reliability, and user experience.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in limited situations:

•                      With trusted third-party service providers (advertising, analytics, cloud storage, payment processing) who assist in operating the Service and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

•                      With ad partners to enable personalized advertising (using IDFA / GAID only).

•                      When required by law, regulation, court order, or government request.

•                      In connection with a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or restructuring, where data may be transferred as a business asset.

•                      In aggregated, anonymized form that cannot identify you personally.

Data Security

We use industry‑standard security measures to protect your information:

•                      Data transmission uses TLS (Transport Layer Security) encryption.

•                      Stored personal data is protected with encryption and strict access controls.

•                      Only authorized personnel may access sensitive data.

•                      Regular security audits and system updates reduce risks.

While we take strong precautions, no electronic transmission or storage method is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:

•                      Account and activity data are kept while your account is active.

•                      After you request account deletion, we immediately remove personal identifiers such as your name, email, and profile picture.

•                      To prevent reward abuse or duplicate payouts, we may temporarily keep non‑identifiable payment records for up to 6 months.

•                      Anonymized analytics data may be retained for product improvement.

Account & Data Deletion

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  1.                 In‑app: Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.
  1.                 By email: Send a request to JoelWyatt6697@icloud.com with the subject line: WalkRush Account Deletion Request.

Please include your registered email for verification. We will process and confirm deletion within 14 business days. Deleted step data, rewards, and account information cannot be recovered.

When the app errors, crashes, or performs poorly, we collect Log Data via third‑party tools. This may include IP address, device model, OS version, app state, error codes, and timestamps. Log Data is used only to diagnose and fix technical issues and does not identify individual users.

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings. Disabling cookies may affect some personalized features but not core step tracking.

With your explicit permission, we may access your contact list only for fraud prevention and optional friend‑invitation functions. If you revoke permission, we delete stored contact data within 3 business days.
